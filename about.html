About Water My Plants

Water My Plants is designed to make creating a watering schedule simple, intuitive, and fun. Forgetting when you last watered a plant is easy to do when you've got so many other tasks jostling for attention. Water too soon and your plant friends get soggy feet, but water too late and they're dying of thirst. Plant care is supposed to be relaxing and rewarding, not anxiety-inducing. Let Water My Plants take the guesswork out of plant care so no plant ever goes unwatered again.
